Macaroni Cheese

Homepage

Recipe Description

This perfect baked macaroni cheese recipe comes with a creamy cheese sauce, a hint of mustard and uses leftover French stick for its crunchy topping

Ingredients

  • 50g baguette, cut into small chunks
  • 2 tbsp butter, plus 1 tbsp melted
  • 350g spiral or other short pasta
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tsp English mustard powder
  • 2 tsp plain flour
  • 500 ml whole milk
  • 250g vegetarian mature cheddar, grated
  • 50g parmesan (or vegetarian alternative), grated

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 200c for convection, 180c for fan
  2. Chop and spread baguette over baking sheet covered in melted butter
  3. Bake baguette for 6 minutes, until crispy
  4. Boil pasta for 2 minutes less than pack states and drain
  5. Melt butter in saucepan, add garlic and mustard, cook for 1 minute then add flour
  6. Cook mixture for one more minutem whisk until lump free
  7. Simmer for 5 minutes while you whisk until thick
  8. Take pan off the heat, then stire in cheddar and half the parmesan
  9. Stir in pasta to the sauce, then put into an ovenproof dish
  10. scatter over bread and remaining cheese before baking for 20 minutes
